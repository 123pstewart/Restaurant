Steak Alfredo

Steak Alfredo features fettuccine noodles coated in a creamy, Parmesan flavored white sauce then topped with perfectly cooked steak. I chose to serve the steak on top of my noodles, but cooked and cubed steak could be tossed with the sauce and the pasta. This dish is simple, yet impressive.
